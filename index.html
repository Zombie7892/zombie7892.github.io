About me

Hello! My name is Kirill. I am 27 y.o. I was born in Novorossiysk. This is very beautiful Russian port placed on the Black Sea coast. Our city is famous for maritime academy and big amount of seamans. This is why I have got a maritime degree as my first specialty. I have visited a lot of countries and saw a lot of different cultures.

I like nature and animals. For me traveling is always connected to the inspiration!

But my main activity is ART! I am drawing since childhood, but with some breaks for few years. During my life I tried drawing and painting with traditional media, digital art, design and illustration, sculpturing, 3D modeling and street art.

My artworks

Birds

Little story behind my drawings of birds: Since some moment of my life I started to draw birds a lot! For me bird is a symbol of Freedom! They have unlimited opportunities to explore the World. As a visual type of person I really like to observe the world around and would like to have same ability to fly and explore the beauty of out planet!

Concept Art

I also really interested in Concept art. Since childhood I really like to invent some interesting ideas and to put them on paper. It is very interesting to give your imagination difficult challenges.
